Background

I am a former History/French teacher, turned Software Engineer, who wants to satisfy my curiousity about all facets of the world by interviewing some of the most interesting people out there.


I have a wide range of skills and expertise but want to meet and chat with people from all over the world and from any field imaginable. The podcast will feature conversations about science, history, language, wisdom, philosophy and much more.


I'm so grateful to all those in my life who have shaped me to the man I am today. Some of the most inspirational moments that have cause the biggest shifts in my mindset, career, and life have come from moments and conversations I hope to experience on the podcast and I hope the listeners can experience those moments with my guests and I. In my free time I like to learn and the podcast will be an extension of that.
