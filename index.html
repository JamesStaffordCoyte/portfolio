About

We have had the privilege to hike many beautiful mountains and we would love to share our past and upcoming adventures with you while helping raise funds for the children of Nepal to have access to mental health treatment and counselling.


Our goal is to hike 3483 meters in elevation (similar to Everest Base Camp to the summit) and to raise the same amount in funds.


We will be posting regularly with updates of new and old hikes. Please donate when you can, any amount is appreciated. Thank you for your support and donations!
