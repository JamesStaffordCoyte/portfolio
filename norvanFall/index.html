Day 2 - Norvan Falls

Distance Elevation Gain Cummulative Gain Time
14.8 km 195 m 248 m 3 h 21 m

It's been a weekend of waterfalls. Today we went for a pleasant hike to Norvan Falls in Lynn Headwaters Regional Park. This trail is very popular with trail runners and dog walkers.

Thoughts and recommendations:

Another family friendly hike. We even saw several families bring (and carry) their little ones! It is heavily trafficked so we definitely recommend waking up early to avoid the crowds. We got to the falls around 9:30am and enjoyed the beautiful views by ourselves. There's also a small suspension bridge that you can check out. Great hike for a rainy day (we got lucky today and stayed dry).
